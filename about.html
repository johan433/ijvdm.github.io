Academic Background

My academic journey spans over three decades, beginning with a BA cum laude (1989) and progressing through a BTh cum laude (1992), MTh cum laude (1994), PhD (1999), and MBA cum laude (2013) from Stellenbosch University.

I have been honored with numerous awards including the Old Mutual Gold Medal Award for exceptional MBA performance and multiple DAAD invitations for research in Germany (1995, 2002, 2007, 2013, 2017).

Research & Academic Role

As a Research Fellow at Stellenbosch University's Systematic Theology and Ethics Research Institute since 2000, I focus on the intersection of ethics, theology, and contemporary issues including AI ethics and pandemic response.

I have supervised PhD and MDiv theses and lectured in Economics and Ethics at Stellenbosch University (2011-2014), as well as ongoing teaching at Communitas covering communication, marketing, liturgy and ethics.

Data Science & AI Strategy

Since 2015, I serve as Data Science Strategist at Praelexis (and Praelexis AI GmbH since 2022), where I identify business cases for advanced analytics and oversee AI solution delivery across diverse sectors.

My focus is on ensuring responsible, explainable, and ethical use of AI, combining technical expertise with ethical frameworks developed through my theological background.

Leadership & Consulting

As Director of the Ascent Group and certified executive coach through multiple organizations (The Leadership Circle, Organic Scorecard, Gallup StrengthsFinder, Keirsey Temperament Sorter), I provide strategic consulting across industries including sports, retail, engineering, wine, and education.

I facilitate workshops on business strategy, leadership development, team synergy, emotional intelligence, and data strategy while serving as Pastor at Dutch Reformed Church Stellenbosch since 2001.
